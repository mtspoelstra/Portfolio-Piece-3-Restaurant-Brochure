Menu

We use only the best plant based, organic ingredients.
Our entire menu is free from refined sugar, soy, corn, gluten and dairy.

Entrees

  • Black Bean Burger

    Burger made in house on homemade seaseme seed bun with tomotaoe, lettuce, cashew cheese and pickles. Topped with homeade spicy cashew aoli-sauce.

  • Cauliflower Wings

    Cauliflower breaded in chick-pea flour, coated with homemade cashew spicy buffalo sauce and fried. Served with a side of homemade cashew blue cheese dip.

  • Caprese Sandwich

    Cashew mozzarella cheese, hemp seed-basil pesto, balsamic reduction, fresh spinach, and tomato on gluten free bread. Served with a side salad.

  • Mexican Style Breakfast Bowl

    Bowl with rice, freshly made pico de gallo, pinto beans, lettuce, guacamole and hot cashew cheese sauce.

  • Sushi Bowl

    White rice topped with crisp cucumbers, nutritious nori, pumfu, and our house made spicy cashew cream sauce.

  • Vegtable Curry Bowl

    White rice topped with fresh vegtables cooked in a coconut cream sauce and warm spices.
